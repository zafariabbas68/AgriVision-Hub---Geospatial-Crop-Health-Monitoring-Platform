ℹ️ About Satellite NDVI

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) measures vegetation health:

  • NDVI > 0.6 - Excellent, very healthy vegetation
  • NDVI 0.4-0.6 - Good, healthy vegetation
  • NDVI 0.2-0.4 - Moderate, stressed vegetation
  • NDVI < 0.2 - Poor, sparse or unhealthy vegetation

Data source: Simulated satellite data based on location characteristics
